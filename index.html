Cybersheild - Social Engineering Attempts detection solution for corporate sector

Research Project Scope

This research focuses on the development of a mobile application-based solution capable of detecting smishing and vishing attacks in real time. The system will analyze incoming SMS and call-related metadata using machine learning and NLP models to identify malicious patterns. It aims to distinguish between legitimate and fraudulent communications, classify threats based on their nature (smishing or vishing), and determine whether they originate from human or AI sources. The application will notify users of potential threats and provide recommended mitigation actions. The scope is limited to mobile devices and does not extend to email-based phishing or desktop platforms.

Research Problem

The growing sophistication of smishing (SMS phishing) and vishing (voice phishing) attacks presents a critical challenge to mobile security. Traditional rule-based and basic machine learning approaches often fail to detect these threats, especially when messages are generated using advanced AI tools that mimic human language patterns. Furthermore, current systems rarely distinguish between AI-generated and human-crafted phishing attempts, leading to misclassification and reduced effectiveness. There is a pressing need for a real-time, mobile-based solution that not only detects smishing and vishing attacks but also identifies the origin of the message content to provide accurate threat categorization and effective user mitigation strategies.

Research Objectives

The primary objective of this research is to develop a mobile application-based system capable of detecting and classifying smishing and vishing attacks, including those generated by AI. The system aims to deliver real-time alerts and recommend mitigation strategies to users based on the type and origin of the threat.

Specific objectives include:

  • To identify key linguistic and behavioral features of smishing and vishing messages, both human- and AI-generated.
  • To build and train an SVM-based model for general smishing detection.
  • To develop a transformer-based classifier for detecting AI-generated smishing content with high accuracy.
  • To create a classification model that categorizes smishing threats into distinct types (e.g., financial fraud, impersonation).
  • To integrate the detection and classification models into a real-time mobile application for end-user protection.
  • To evaluate the system’s performance in terms of accuracy, response time, and usability on mobile platforms.

Component 1

Implementing a Vishing Analysis and Detection Method – Analyzing Call Content, Voice Patterns, and Behavioral Indicators.

Component 2

Implementing a Real-Time Emotion Detection System in Voice Calls Using Machine learning Algorithms and Features.

Component 3

Implementing a Message Content Analyzer to identify the Smishing attempts by the message content including AI generated content and give mitigation techniques

Component 4​

Implementing a Smishing URL Scanner with Content Analysis, Domain Credibility, and Context-aware Evaluation.

Exploring the Advanced Capabilities of the Cybersheild social engineering attempt detection solution: A Comprehensive Demonstration

The main objectives of our proposed mobile application are,

  • Implementing a Vishing Analysis and Detection Method – Analyzing Call Content & Voice.
  • Analyzing callers' emotions in real-time by analyzing voice tones and speech patterns
  • Implementing a Message Content Analyzer to identify the Smishing attempts and classify them as AI generated or human generated
  • Implementing a Smishing URL Scanner with Content Analysis, Domain Credibility, and Context-aware Evaluation.

Our cutting-edge mobile app transforms mobile security by analyzing URLs and message content to detect smishing attempts, while also identifying vishing attacks through voice recognition technology. With an intuitive user interface and robust AI detection, it empowers users to stay safe from phishing and vishing threats effortlessly.
